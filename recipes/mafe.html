Maffé

Description

Peanut sauce is a sauce originating from Mali, mainly used in West Africa. It is known as Mafé in Mali and Senegal, Azindezzi in Togo, Aziin Nusunnu in Benin, Peanuts sauce in Ghana and Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • Sugar
  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • pepper
  • Salt
  • onion
  • free-range chicken thighs
  • 3 sweet potatoes
  • 2 slices of cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Peel and mince the onions.
  2. brown the chicken in the oil in a "casserole" for 10 minutes and set aside.
  3. lower the heat under the "cocotte" and add the onions.
  4. in a small bowl, combine the tomato puree, sugar, water, salt and pepper as well as the chilli.
  5. Pour the mixture over the onions and mix.
  6. add the peanut butter and mix again, .
  7. return the chicken to the casserole dish and mix one last time, leaving it over medium heat.
  8. pour boiling water (a good bottom but not level) on the preparation and leave to simmer for 35 minutes without covering.
  9. meanwhile, wash the sweet potatoes and cut them into 4 or 5 pieces depending on their size. Steam them skin on for 30 mins maximum.
  10. once cooked, peel the potatoes and serve them as an accompaniment to the chicken.
